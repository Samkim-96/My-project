About IYF Kenya

IYF Kenya was established as a worldwide youth organization and international NGO based on a Christian mindset in 2001. Our mission is to raise leaders with a global insight through youth education, activities, and provide realistic solutions to problems in the global village.

Our Values

  • Change: A true change of life that begins from one's heart and then brings change to the world.
  • Challenge: A true challenge to overcome one's limitations and experience endless possibilities.
  • Cohesion: A true cohesive mind that transcends ethnicity, nationality, language, and religion.

IYF and Kenyan Government Cooperation

The goverment of Kenya and iyf kenya have good mutual relationship through the ministry of youth Affairs .
This has seen Government and IYF kenya signing MOU agreement to assist the government in implenting the mindset education to the public servant and its implentation in the new curriculum.
Through the ministry of youth affairs and IYF-kenya, the organisation is conducting free weekend academy to kenyans youths to gain hand on skills for self employment.
This is a great cooperation that will benefits many youth to secure employments in future and do develop themselve and society in general through Mindset Education
